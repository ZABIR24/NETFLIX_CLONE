  • Netflix subscription plans in India start at Rs. 149 per month for the Mobile plan, which allows you to watch on one device in SD quality.   • You can watch Netflix on a variety of devices, including smart TVs, streaming devices, Blu-ray players, game consoles, laptops, computers, and smartphones and tablets. Netflix is also available on some airplanes and hotels.
